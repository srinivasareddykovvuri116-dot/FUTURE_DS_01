# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to identify important business insights and performance trends. The analysis helps understand product performance, customer behavior, regional sales distribution, and overall business growth.

The project was completed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship at Future Interns.

## Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-selling product categories
- Analyze regional sales performance
- Study monthly sales trends
- Identify high-value customers

- Generate business insights and recommendations

# Analysis Performed

## 1. Product Performance Analysis

Analyzed product categories to identify the highest and lowest revenue-generating products.

### Findings
- Classic Cars generated the highest sales revenue.
- Trains generated the lowest sales revenue.

---

## 2. Regional Sales Analysis

Performed country-wise sales analysis to identify strong and weak performing regions.

### Findings
- Certain countries contributed significantly higher revenue compared to others.
- Regional sales distribution was uneven across markets.

---

## 3. Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

Analyzed sales performance across different months to identify seasonal trends.

### Findings
- November recorded the highest sales.
- June recorded the lowest sales.

---

## 4. Customer Analysis

Identified top revenue-generating customers.

### Findings
- A small number of customers contributed a major share of total revenue.
- Euro Shopping Channel was among the highest-value customers.

---

## 5. KPI Metrics Analysis

Calculated important business KPIs including:
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Sales Per Order
- Product Category Count

---

## 6. Yearly Sales Analysis

Compared yearly sales performance to identify business growth trends.

### Findings
- Sales performance varied significantly across years.

---

## 7. Deal Size Analysis

Analyzed revenue contribution by deal sizes.

### Findings
- Medium-sized deals contributed the highest revenue share.

---

# Business Insights

1. Classic Cars generated the highest sales revenue among all product categories, indicating strong customer demand.

2. Trains recorded the lowest sales performance, suggesting lower customer interest.

3. November achieved the highest monthly sales, indicating strong seasonal demand near year-end.

4. High-value customers contribute a major portion of company revenue.

5. Medium-sized deals generated the highest revenue contribution.

---

# Business Recommendations

1. Increase marketing investment for high-performing categories such as Classic Cars.

2. Improve promotional strategies for low-performing categories like Trains.

3. Build loyalty programs for high-value customers.

4. Prepare inventory and advertisements before peak months like November and December.

5. Focus expansion strategies on high-performing regions.

---

# Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics techniques can be used to extract meaningful business insights from raw sales data. The analysis supports data-driven decision-making and helps businesses improve sales performance, customer retention, and strategic planning.
